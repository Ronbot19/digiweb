What is Digital Literacy?

Digital literacy is the ability to use technology to navigate, evaluate, and create information. It is essential for everyone in today's world to be digitally literate, whether for personal, educational, or professional purposes.

At Digital Literacy, we aim to make digital skills accessible to all, from basic computer skills to advanced digital tools for the modern world.

About Us

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing individuals and communities with the necessary digital skills for success in the 21st century. We advocate for digital inclusion and work towards creating a future where everyone has the skills to thrive in an increasingly digital world.

Whether you're a beginner or looking to improve your skills, we provide free resources, articles, tutorials, and a community to help you on your digital literacy journey.

Resources

We offer a variety of free resources designed to help you improve your digital skills:

  • Beginner Guides: Learn the basics of using computers, the internet, and social media.
  • Video Tutorials: Watch step-by-step videos to get hands-on experience with digital tools.
  • Printable Guides: Access downloadable PDFs and eBooks on digital literacy topics.
  • Webinars: Join live sessions to learn from experts in the digital literacy field.
